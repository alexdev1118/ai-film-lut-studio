## Classification policy

- FAIL: a technical round-trip mismatch, severe clipping growth, severe skin/neutral drift, or severe red-risk ratio.
- WARNING: the technical chain is correct, but at least one real scene crosses a conservative creative-risk threshold.
- PASS: technical consistency is validated and no conservative creative-risk threshold is crossed.
- Source PREs and all DaVinci renders remain under `local-test-assets/S17.3/` and are excluded from Git.
